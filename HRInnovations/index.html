The Challenge

Social care is drowning in paperwork and missing what matters.

Social care organisations operate in a highly regulated environment where safeguarding, incident reporting, and operational documentation must be maintained to a consistent standard.

However, many organisations continue to rely on manual reporting systems, inconsistent staff documentation, paper-based or fragmented records, and administrative processes that place additional pressure on frontline staff.

Loss Relief

Inheritance Tax Relief

Important Risk Information

Key risks you should understand

Due to the potential for losses, the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) considers this investment to be high risk.

1

You could lose all the money you invest

If the business fails, you are likely to lose 100% of the money you invested. Most start-up businesses fail.

2

You are unlikely to be protected if something goes wrong

Protection from the FSCS does not cover poor investment performance. Protection from the FOS does not cover poor investment performance.

3

You won't get your money back quickly

Even if successful, it may take several years to get your money back. You are unlikely to be able to sell your investment early.

4

Don't put all your eggs in one basket

A good rule of thumb is not to invest more than 10% of your money in high-risk investments.

5

The value of your investment can be reduced

Your ownership percentage will decrease if the business issues more shares. Most start-ups issue multiple rounds.

If you are interested in learning more about how to protect yourself, visit the FCA's website at fca.org.uk/investsmart.
