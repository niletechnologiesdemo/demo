About

LayBys.com is Australia's dedicated lay-by marketplace — bringing back the trusted, transparent way to pay without credit checks, interest, or hidden fees.

Our Mission

Everyone deserves access to products without being pushed into debt. Lay-by is the original "pay over time" system.

For Merchants

Simple, automated platform to offer lay-by. List products, set terms, let the system handle the rest.

How It Works

For Customers

Browse products, check lay-by terms upfront, pay deposit to reserve, instalments charged automatically. Once paid in full, product ships.

For Merchants

Onboarded by admin. List products, set pricing and lay-by terms. Platform handles collection, reminders, and tracking.

Lay-By vs BNPL

With BNPL you get the product and owe money. With lay-by, you pay first. No debt, no credit checks.

LayBy Laws

Lay-by agreements in Australia are governed by state and territory consumer protection laws.

Key Rights

Clear written terms required. Price locked in. Right to cancel anytime. Reasonable termination fee may apply. Merchant must store item safely.

Privacy

We collect information you provide when creating an account and usage data. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Security

Payment processing handled by PCI-compliant third-party providers. We do not store credit card numbers.
