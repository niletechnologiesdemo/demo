Product Description

The Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra is the most powerful Galaxy smartphone ever made. Featuring a stunning 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with 120Hz adaptive refresh rate, it delivers an immersive visual experience for everything from gaming to streaming.

Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite processor with 12GB RAM, it handles everything you throw at it with ease. The quad-camera system features a 200MP main sensor, 50MP periscope telephoto (5x optical zoom), 50MP telephoto (3x), and 12MP ultra-wide — making it the best camera phone on the market.

With built-in S Pen, 5,000mAh battery with 45W fast charging, IP68 water resistance, and titanium frame, this is a device built to last. Available in Titanium Grey, Titanium Blue, Titanium Silver, and Titanium Black.
