What happens at SLA breach

If the SLA window expires without action:

  1. LayBys.com Support is auto-notified and may contact the customer on your behalf.
  2. The lay-by enters a 30-day final recovery window with weekly courtesy retries.
  3. If still unrecovered after 30 days, the lay-by is auto-cancelled. The customer is refunded their instalments minus the deposit, and the stock returns to your inventory.
  4. This event is recorded against the customer's payment score but does not affect your store rating.
