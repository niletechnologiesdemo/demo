How disputes start
A customer files a dispute via the LayBys.com app. You'll get notified within minutes and have 7 days to respond with evidence (proof of dispatch, condition photos, communication logs).
Evidence that wins
Tracking number with proof-of-delivery, photos taken at dispatch, and your reply timestamps in the customer's chat thread. The platform stores all of this automatically.
Resolution timeline
Most disputes resolve in 5–10 business days. The LayBys.com team reviews evidence from both sides and decides. Funds remain in escrow until resolution.
