  • Locked consoles and scheduled pickups for offices
  • Industry-specific programmes for healthcare, legal, financial, and education

About

Care, coordination, and recovery — together.

LegalMed Liaison Network is a trusted organisation based in Palm Beach County, Florida, serving Palm Beach, Broward, and Miami-Dade counties. We connect injured clients with top doctors and lawyers, providing comprehensive case management, medical coordination, and legal support to ensure clients receive the best care and representation during their recovery.

Visit LegalMed →
What We Do

Healthcare and legal coordination — collaborating for better outcomes.

A trusted liaison between injured clients, treating providers, and personal-injury counsel — keeping every case moving and every file in order.

  • Vetted network of specialist doctors and personal-injury attorneys
  • End-to-end case management and medical record coordination
  • Settlement preparation, lien reductions, and paralegal support
  • Compliance guidance across healthcare and legal frameworks
