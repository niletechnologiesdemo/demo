Which choice most effectively maintains the essay's tone?
A
NO CHANGE
B
foster historical exponents
C
locate countrywide profusion
D
extend beyond the local level
Subject: ACT English | Unit: Knowledge of Language | Topic: Style (Tone, Clarity, Variety)
Explanation

Choice D is the best answer because "extend beyond the local level" is a neutral, narrative description of scope. It thereby maintains the balanced, descriptive tone of the essay.

Choice A is incorrect because "nationalism" is negative, which does not match the balanced tone. Choice B is incorrect because "foster historical exponents" is too academic. Choice C is incorrect because "locate countrywide profusion" is too scientific.

The Need for Repurposed Water Bottles

Residents of the US have been known to use about 29 billion plastic water bottles in one year, more than are used by inhabitants of any other country in the world, but recycle only about 23% of these disposable bottles. Concerned individuals have attempted local solutions: water filling stations proliferate in schools, shopping centers, museums, and airports.

Compounding the problem. Plastic waste often escapes landfills and accumulates in waterways and oceans with horrific results. One of these, the "Great Pacific Garbage Patch," is a collection of plastic trash floating between California and Hawaii.

Fortunately, name-brand companies are attempting to counter water bottle waste not by simply sequestering bottles but with entire product lines that are crafted to repurpose water bottle plastic.

Environmental stewardship requires individual and collective action. Small adjustments to the way we live can help to confront problems such as water bottle waste.

Which choice makes the sentence most grammatically acceptable?
A.
NO CHANGE
B.
solutions, water
C.
solutions and water
D.
solutions. When water
