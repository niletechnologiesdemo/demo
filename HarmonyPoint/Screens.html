What is Mediation?
A Complete Guide

By Harmony Point Team · 8 min read · Apr 22, 2026

Mediation is a structured process where an impartial third party helps disputing parties reach a mutually acceptable agreement. Unlike courtroom litigation, mediation emphasizes cooperation, communication, and creative problem-solving.

How Does Mediation Work?

The mediator does not make decisions or impose solutions. Instead, they facilitate dialogue between both parties, helping them identify shared interests and explore options for resolution.

Key benefits include lower costs, faster resolution, confidentiality, and preserved relationships — especially important when children are involved.
