What is Mediation? A Complete Guide

By Harmony Point Team · 8 min read · Apr 22, 2026

Mediation is a structured process where an impartial third party helps disputing parties reach a mutually acceptable agreement. Unlike litigation, mediation emphasizes cooperation and problem-solving.

How Does Mediation Work?

The mediator facilitates dialogue, helping identify shared interests. They do not make decisions or impose solutions.

Key benefits: lower costs, faster resolution, confidentiality, and preserved relationships.
