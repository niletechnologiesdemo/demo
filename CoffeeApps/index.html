About

The Peacemaker Coffee Story

Clean Coffee, Community Mission
Our Story — Toledo, Ohio

Why We Roast Mold-Free Coffee

Founded by now-retired police officer/chief Chris Morris after 20+ years of dedicated service, Peacemaker Coffee Company is a proud family-owned and operated business. We roast our beans right here in Toledo, Ohio.

Every bag we sell supports donations of coffee to first responders, veterans, educators, and frontline workers. Because exceptional coffee should fuel those who protect and care for us all.

Our Values

Faith, Family & Community — Rooted in service and togetherness.
Support for Heroes — Coffee donations to police, fire, military, EMS, dispatchers, teachers, veterans.
Transparency & Quality — 3rd-party lab-tested for mold, mycotoxins, and contaminants.
Health-Focused — Low-acidity, regeneratively farmed, SCA score 82+ specialty grade.
Ethically Sourced — Brazil, Colombia, Guatemala, Uganda via direct/fair trade.

App Features

Everything on peacemakercoffeecompany.com — now in your pocket
🛒

Full Product Catalog

All coffees (pods, bags, flavored), apparel, roaster supplies, gift cards — browsable with filters by category, roast type, and format.

📦

VIP Autoship Management

Subscribers manage their plan: swap roasts, switch between ground/whole bean, skip a month, adjust bag count, or cancel — all from the app. Exact plans from the website.

⭐

Loyalty Rewards Program

5 points per $1 spent. 1,000 points = 15% off coffee. 50 points on sign-up. Fully integrated with the existing website loyalty system.

👥

Refer Friends

1,000 loyalty rewards per referral. Friend gets 15% off their first order. Shareable referral link directly from the app.

🔔

Push Notifications

New roast launches, flash sales, VIP shipment reminders, reorder nudges, loyalty point milestones, blog post alerts.

📰

Blog & Resource Center

All blog articles from the website — mold-free coffee education, health resources, FAQ, brewing tips — available in-app.

🏪

Retail Store Locator

Full retail location directory organized by state — matching the existing Retail page. Customers find local stores carrying Peacemaker Coffee.

🎁

Gift Cards

Purchase digital gift cards ($25, $50, $75, $100+) directly in the app. Send to friends with a personalized message.
