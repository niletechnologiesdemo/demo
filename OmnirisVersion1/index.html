Landy D. Barthelemy

Founder & Managing Director

Landy D. Barthelemy, MBA, MHA, is the Founder and Managing Director of Omniris Holdings, a multi-industry organization focused on building and managing scalable, compliance-driven businesses. With over 15 years of experience in the legal field, Landy has developed deep expertise in litigation support, case management, and operational systems. Her work has consistently centered on improving efficiency, ensuring regulatory compliance, and delivering structured solutions within complex environments.

She holds a Bachelor in Legal Studies, a Master of Business Administration (MBA) and a Master of Healthcare Administration (MHA), allowing her to bridge the gap between legal, healthcare, and business operations with a strategic and results-oriented approach. Through Omniris Holdings, Landy leads a portfolio of companies that operate both independently and collaboratively — delivering integrated services across document security, insurance solutions, and legal-medical coordination. Her leadership is defined by a commitment to structure, scalability, and long-term value creation, with a focus on building systems that support both operational excellence and sustainable growth.

Omniris Holdings reflects her vision of creating a unified platform that delivers clarity, efficiency, and growth across multiple industries.
